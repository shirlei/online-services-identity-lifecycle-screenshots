*Steps performed via a web browser.*

### Creating a local account

1 - Access to the initial page ([1-linkedin-create-account.png](./create/1-linkedin-create-account.png)).

2 - After clicking on *Join Now* on step 1, fill up the first part of the signup form ([2-linkedin-create-account.png](./create/2-linkedin-create-account.png)).

3 - Fill up the second part of the signup form ([3-linkedin-create-account.png](./create/3-linkedin-create-account.png)).

4 - Fill up the third part of the signup form ([4-linkedin-create-account.png](./create/4-linkedin-create-account.png)).

5 - Fill up the fourth part of the signup form ([5-linkedin-create-account.png](./create/5-linkedin-create-account.png)).

6 - Fill up the fifth part of the signup form - in this case having hit *I'm a student* on step 5 ([6-linkedin-create-account.png](./create/6-linkedin-create-account.png)).

7 - Inform the verification code sent to your email([7-linkedin-create-account.png](./create/7-linkedin-create-account.png)).

8 - Inform if you are looking for a job ([8-linkedin-create-account.png](./create/8-linkedin-create-account.png)).

9 - Add a photo or skip ([9-linkedin-create-account.png](./create/9-linkedin-create-account.png)).

10 - Download the app for mobile devices by reading the QR code and/or click *Next* ([10-linkedin-create-account.png](./create/10-linkedin-create-account.png)).

11 - Click *Finish* to complete the account creation ([11-linkedin-create-account.png](./create/11-linkedin-create-account.png)).


### Creating a federated account

1 - Access the initial page and click on *Join now* ([1-linkedin-create-account.png](./create/1-linkedin-create-account.png)).

2 - Click on *Continue with Google* ([2-linkedin-create-account.png](./create/2-linkedin-create-account.png)).

3 - Select the Google account you want to use ([01-linkedin-create-account-fed.png](./create/01-linkedin-create-account-fed.png)).

4 - On the next page, the personal details that Google will share with LinkedIn are shown. Click on *Confirm* to proceed with the sharing ([02-linkedin-create-account-fed.png](./create/02-linkedin-create-account-fed.png)).

5 - Click on *Agree and Join* ([03-linkedin-create-account-fed.png](./create/03-linkedin-create-account-fed.png)).

6 - Fill up your location data ([04-linkedin-create-account-fed.png](./create/04-linkedin-create-account-fed.png)).

7 - Inform your job title or select *I'm a student* ([5-linkedin-create-account.png](./create/5-linkedin-create-account.png)).

8 - Fill up school/university information - in this case having hit *I'm a student* on step 7 ([6-linkedin-create-account.png](./create/6-linkedin-create-account.png)).

9 - Inform if you are looking for a job ([8-linkedin-create-account.png](./create/8-linkedin-create-account.png)).

10 - Download the app for mobile devices by reading the QR code and/or clicking _Next_ ([10-linkedin-create-account.png](./create/10-linkedin-create-account.png)).

11 - Click *Finish* to complete the account creation ([11-linkedin-create-account.png](./create/11-linkedin-create-account.png)).

### Usage and Maintenance

1 - After accessing the initial page, click on *Continue with Google* or *Sign in with email* ([01-linkedin-account-login.png](./usage/01-linkedin-account-login.png)).

2 - If the user has a service-specific account, he or she chooses to _Sign in_ with email* and enters his/her username (email) and password ([02-linkedin-account-login.png](./usage/02-linkedin-account-login.png)).

3 - If the user has a third-party sign-in account, he/she chooses to _Continue with_ Google* and on the new windows, chooses the Google account to sign in ([03-linkedin-account-login.png](./usage/03-linkedin-account-login.png)). If the selected Google account is not logged in, he/she will be asked by Google to log in.

4 - After logging in either via steps 2 or 3, the user is redirected to the initial page. On the top right menu, there is a profile (named "Me") icon where the user can click to expand and access management options such as "View Profile" and "Settings and Privacy" ([04-linkedin-account-manage.png](./usage/04-linkedin-account-manage.png)).

5 - On the "View Profile" page, the user can edit his/her public profile, including professional experience, academic details, experience, skills and others ([05-linkedin-account-manage.png](./usage/05-linkedin-account-manage.png)).

6 - On the "Settings & Privacy" page, the user can edit account preferences like Profile Information, general information and others ([06-linkedin-account-manage.png](./usage/06-linkedin-account-manage.png)).

7 - On the "Profile Information" Section, *Name, location, and industry*, the user can edit name and pronoun information ([07-linkedin-account-manage.png](./usage/07-linkedin-account-manage.png)).


8 - On the "Profile Information" Section, *Personal demographic information*, the user can edit information on gender and disabilities ([08-linkedin-account-manage.png](./usage/08-linkedin-account-manage.png)).

9 - On the "Sign in & security" option of the Settings & Privacy page, the user can change email addresses, phone numbers, password, configure passkeys, see information on where the user is signed in, devices that remember his/her password and configure Two-step verification ([09-linkedin-account-manage.png](./usage/09-linkedin-account-manage.png)).

10 - On the "Visibility" option of the Settings & Privacy page, the user can manage several settings related to the visibility of his/her account, like profile viewing options, connections, who can see his/her email address, follows, connections, last name, profile discovery, visibility of activity, enable that other users tag him/her and others ([10-linkedin-account-manage.png](./usage/10-linkedin-account-manage.png)).

11 - On the "Data privacy" option of the Settings & Privacy page, the user has some shortcuts to settings available on Visibility, Sign in & security and Account Preferences. He or she can also see search history and request a copy of his/her data by clicking on *Get a copy of your data* in the section "How Linkedin uses your data" ([11-linkedin-account-manage.png](./usage/11-linkedin-account-manage.png)).

12 - After clicking on *Get a copy of your _data_, on the new page, select the required options and click on *Request archive* ([12-linkedin-account-manage.png](./usage/12-linkedin-account-manage.png)).

13 - After clicking on *Request archive*, a new page informs that it will be sent an email when the download is ready ([13-linkedin-account-manage.png](./usage/13-linkedin-account-manage.png)).

### Account deletion

1 - After being signed in and clicking on the profile icon and choosing the option _Settings & Privacy_, in the settings page, menu Account preferences and section "Account management", the user can choose to hibernate or to close the account ([01-linkedin-account-delete.png](./deletion/01-linkedin-account-delete.png)).

2 - Click on *Close account* and then in the new window, on *Continue* ([02-linkedin-account-delete.png](./deletion/02-linkedin-account-delete.png)).

3 - On the next page select the reason for closing the account ([03-linkedin-account-delete.png](./deletion/03-linkedin-account-delete.png)).

4 - Inform your password and click _Done_* ([04-linkedin-account-delete.png](./deletion/04-linkedin-account-delete.png)).

5 - The user can also hibernate the account by choosing the _Hibernate__ account_ option sin step 1. On the new window, read the hibernation conditions and click on *Hibernate* ([05-linkedin-account-hibernate.png](./deletion/05-linkedin-account-hibernate.png)).
